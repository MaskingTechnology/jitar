Meet Jitar – an open-source, distributed runtime for JavaScript and TypeScript. With simple configuration, turn your full-stack monolithic apps into a distributed system with seamless frontend-backend integration, type-safe sharing, and built-in features like routing, load balancing, and monitoring. Perfect for developers and architects looking to build powerful, modular, and distributed applications without being locked into a specific framework.
